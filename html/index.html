Bursts is an online platform designed for writing out your feelings and bursts of emotions. Unlike any regular notes application, Bursts deletes your note after 24 hours, to leave you with a fresh start, a blank canvas to write out your thoughts and emotions on a new day. To ensure the utmost security and to preserve the privacy of our users, Bursts requires new users to create an account in order to be able to write and access their private notes.

In this day and age, many people of different demographics, especially teenagers, struggle with mental health issues such as depression, anxiety, etc.... Research has shown that many individuals struggling with mental health issues use writing as a coping method. Therefore, we have created Bursts, to help those struggling find an outlet to express their emotions using a safe and secure platform.

About us

Bursts is a notes application specfically catered to individuals suffering with mental health issues. Write out your Bursts of emotions and watch them disappear 24 hours later, leaving you with a fresh start to your new day.
